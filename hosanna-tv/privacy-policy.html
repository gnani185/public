Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Hosanna TV ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Hosanna TV mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Hosanna TV does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or login credentials.

We do not require users to create accounts or sign in.

2. Automatically Collected Information

We use trusted third-party services that may collect limited, non-personal information for app functionality and improvement:

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand app usage

This data is anonymous and used only to improve app performance and reliability.

3. Push Notifications

Hosanna TV sends push notifications related to:

  • Live services
  • Church announcements
  • General ministry updates

You can enable or disable notifications at any time from the app settings.

4. Children’s Privacy

Hosanna TV is intended for a general audience of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

6. External Links

The app may contain links to external services such as YouTube. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services.
